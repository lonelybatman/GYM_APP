- **Attribut: `Pad_Type`** (Select/Dropdown)
    
    - Werte: `backrest`, `sit`, `shoulder_pad`, `thigh_pad`, `sit_thigh_pad`, `none`
        
- **Attribut: `Action_Point`** (Select/Dropdown)
    
    - Werte: `grip`, `foot_platform`, `roller`, `pad`
        
- **Attribut: `Grip_Position`** (Select/Dropdown)
    
    - Werte: `front`, `rear`, `side`, `overhead`, `shoulderheight`, `n/a`
        
- **Attribut: `Joints_Count`** (Number/Select)
    
    - Werte: `1 (Simple)`, `2 (Complex)`
